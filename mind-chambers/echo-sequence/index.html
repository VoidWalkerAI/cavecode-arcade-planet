How to Play

Echo Sequence is a pattern memory game:

  • Press Start to watch the pads flash.
  • Wait until it says Your turn.
  • Tap the pads back in the exact same order.
  • Each round adds one more step to the sequence.

If you tap the wrong pad, the run ends, but your best level remains.
