Controls & Rules

Echo Sequence is a simple pattern memory run:

  • Tap Start Run and watch the pads flash.
  • Wait for Your turn, then tap the pads in the same order.
  • Each correct round adds one more step to the pattern.
  • One wrong pad ends the run, but your best level stays.
Match clean echoes, ride the tiers, and light up the grid. Forged in CaveCode Arcade Planet · SageWire Syndicate.
